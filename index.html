About OutfitGen

Your AI-powered style companion

OutfitGen is a smart outfit generator designed to help you discover fresh, coordinated looks in seconds. Whether you're dressing for a casual day out, a formal event, or anything in between, OutfitGen takes the guesswork out of styling.

How It Works

Simply choose a style that matches your mood — casual, sporty, formal, streetwear, and more — and hit generate. Our intelligent matching algorithm pairs tops, bottoms, and footwear into cohesive outfits tailored to your selected vibe.

Why OutfitGen?

  • Effortless styling — No more standing in front of your closet wondering what goes together.
  • Multiple styles — From laid-back casual to polished formal, we cover a wide range of aesthetics.
  • Instant inspiration — Generate as many outfits as you like and save your favorites for later.
  • No account needed — Start generating outfits right away, completely free.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to feel confident in what they wear. OutfitGen was built to make personal styling accessible, fun, and easy — whether you're a fashion enthusiast or someone who just wants to look good without the hassle.

OutfitGen is designed with privacy in mind. The application runs entirely in your browser, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:
